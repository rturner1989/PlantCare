Plant Detail structure

Portrait hero

Forest gradient card with Monty in his current visual state, Fraunces italic name, species, and personality pill. Gentle sway animation (2°) makes him feel alive without being distracting.

Voice quote card

The plant's current in-character voice pulled out as a quote block, Fraunces italic 15px, left-border in leaf green. The "content today" meta shows his current emotional state.

Four care rings

Light / Water / Mood / Feed. Leaf green when fine, coral when low. Percentages in the centre. The Mood ring is the compound "tamagotchi happiness" metric — combines all other factors.

Four quick actions

Water (primary, leaf gradient), Feed, Photo, Diagnose. Water is the most common action and gets the prominent treatment. Diagnose opens the AI first-aid camera flow.

Tabbed sections

History (care log) / Photos (journal) / Care (schedule + recalculate) / Species (reference info). Segmented control style matches the Garden view toggle.

Care log entries

Each log row: icon, action, plant voice at the time, source badge (MANUAL / SENSOR / SIRI), timestamp. Shows provenance for when Phase 2 automation arrives.
