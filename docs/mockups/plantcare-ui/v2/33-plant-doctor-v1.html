Most likely: underwatering

High confidence · 2 symptoms matched

Monty is probably thirsty, not sick.

Yellowing leaves plus crispy edges on a Monstera almost always means underwatering. Your schedule shows it's been 10 days since last water — 3 days overdue for this plant in Medium light.

Try this first — quick fixes

  • Water thoroughly. Until it drains out the bottom. Monstera roots want a deep drink, not a sip.
  • Trim the crispy tips with sharp scissors — they won't recover, but the leaf will.
  • Move away from direct heat — radiator, vent, sunny window. Crisping speeds up there.
  • Check again in 48h. If new leaves look worse, it's not underwatering — come back and refine symptoms.

Plant Doctor locked — hybrid

Ships valuable today (symptom picker + article lookup + quick-fix checklist), gets smarter on Phase 2.5 (AI chat unlocks inside the same shell). One surface, one user-learned flow, no rework needed when the bot arrives.

  • Entry points. Plant Detail radial wheel · Encyclopedia (Plant Doctor tab) · direct search "why are my leaves yellow" redirects here. Any path pre-fills the plant context if available, else shows a plant-picker.
  • Two-step dialog shell. Same primitive as Add Plant (mockup 27). Desktop 640×700 centered dialog, mobile Vaul bottom sheet ~86dvh. Header has a 🩺 medallion + eyebrow ("Plant Doctor · step N") + Fraunces italic title.
  • Step 1 — symptom picker. 9 canonical symptom chips (yellowing · drooping · crispy edges · mouldy soil · pests · webs/sticky · stunted · soft stem · something else). Tap up to 3. Selected chips mint-filled with emerald border + ✓ badge. Photo-assist prompt (optional — attaches to Journal, sets up Phase-2 AI recognition).
  • Step 2 — diagnosis + fixes. Top card: confidence pill + Fraunces italic cause + plant-state-aware summary. Below: mint action-list (3-4 quick fixes). Below: matched articles (ranked by symptom overlap, links to Encyclopedia). Bottom: Ask-the-Doctor CTA (gated).
  • Confidence levels. High (2+ symptoms clearly match one cause) · Medium (1 symptom, multiple candidates) · Low (ambiguous — recommend reading matched articles + trying a photo or asking the bot). Shown as sunshine-tinted pill.
  • Plant-state-aware diagnosis. Doctor reads the plant's actual schedule + environment: "your schedule shows it's been 10 days since last water — 3 days overdue for this plant in Medium light". Not a static article lookup — uses user data. This is Phase 1 value, no LLM required.
  • "Ask the Doctor" = Phase 2.5 gated. Dark-forest gradient card with 🎭 personality-voiced avatar + 🔒 Phase 2.5 lock-pill. When the user upgrades, the CTA opens a chat with Monty (or whichever plant) voiced by Claude via the plant's personality prompt. Fine to ship the dialog without this panel — it's additive.
  • Log to Journal. Primary CTA commits a "Doctor consultation" journal entry (system event type) logging the symptoms + diagnosis + action plan. Feeds the rescue-streak game-loop + lets users look back at what was tried.
  • Offline-safe. Symptom matching + article lookup work offline (symptom→article maps cached). Only the AI-chat CTA requires network. Fails gracefully — "you're offline · articles still work".
  • Copy doesn't panic. "Monty is probably thirsty, not sick" reads calmly. "Try this first" not "Urgent action required". Plant Doctor is reassurance + guidance, not alarm.
  • A11y. Symptom cards are real buttons with aria-pressed for selected state. Confidence pill has aria-label spelling out "High confidence, 2 of 2 symptoms matched". Article cards are links with descriptive aria-labels.
