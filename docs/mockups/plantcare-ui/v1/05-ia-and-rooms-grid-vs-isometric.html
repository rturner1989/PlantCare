PHASE 3Rooms · isometric house

Grid vs Isometric on mobile

MVPGrid

  • Scannable — you see all rooms at a glance in 2 seconds
  • Scales to 8+ rooms without getting cramped
  • Attention badges are unmissable
  • Plant dots give per-room collection preview
  • Ships in a weekend — no 3D work, no custom illustration
  • Less distinctive — looks like most room-based apps
  • Doesn't show spatial relationships between rooms

PHASE 3Isometric house

  • Visually unforgettable — the screenshot that sells the app
  • Plants are literally where they live — spatial memory matches reality
  • Personality system gets ambient expression (a dramatic plant could wilt visibly in its room)
  • Natural upgrade path to interactive 3D (pan, zoom, click-through)
  • Illustration-heavy — every room needs custom SVG
  • Struggles past ~6 rooms; needs scrolling or zoom
  • Attention signals are subtler than badges
Recommendation: Ship the grid for MVP. It's the honest answer — it gets plant care into your hands faster, scales to any number of rooms, and leaves the isometric house as a Phase 3 hero moment where it deserves dedicated illustration time. The IA stays identical between both views, so the isometric becomes a toggle inside Garden alongside "Rooms grid" and "List view" — three ways to look at the same data. That's the "view toggle" pill you can see at the top of both phones.
