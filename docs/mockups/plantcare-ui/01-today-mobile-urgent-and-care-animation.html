The care animation sequence

Framer Motion · ~1.8s total

When you tap "Water Monty", five things happen in sequence. This is what gives the care loop its weight — what Tamagotchi had that made you want to come back. It's the emotional reward for doing the thing.

0.0s · Tap

Haptic + ripple

Light haptic. Button scales 0.95. Ripple from touch point.

0.2s · Pour

Water drops fall

4–6 drops rain from top with stagger. Soil darkens.

0.6s · Morph

SVG interpolates

Paths tween from wilting → upright over 600ms with spring easing.

1.2s · Reward

Sparkles + wiggle

Gold sparkles burst outward. Plant does a small celebration bounce.

1.6s · Settle

Thriving state

Quote fades in. Progress ring tweens 1/3 → 2/3. Task row collapses to done.

Same tap, five reactions
